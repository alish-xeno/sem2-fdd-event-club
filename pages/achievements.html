Milestones & Achievements

Key moments in our journey from 2021 to present

2025

Reached 800+ Active Members

  • Surpassed 800 registered members milestone in January
  • Launched mentorship program connecting seniors with freshmen
  • Partnered with Microsoft and Google as platinum sponsors
  • Organized first-ever 3-day Web Development Bootcamp
2024

Expanded to Regional Recognition

  • Won "Best Student Club" at APU Excellence Awards
  • Annual Hackathon attracted 80 participants from 5 universities
  • Hosted 30+ workshops with 2000+ total attendees
  • Established partnerships with 15+ tech companies
  • Launched alumni network with 200+ former members
2023

Solidified Campus Presence

  • Reached 500 active members - doubling from previous year
  • Introduced certification program for workshop participants
  • Organized first annual career fair with 20+ companies
  • Received "Outstanding Hackathon Organization" award
  • Launched official website and social media channels
2022

First Year Achievements

  • Grew from 50 founding members to 250+ within 8 months
  • Successfully organized 15+ events and workshops
  • Partnered with GitHub Education and AWS Educate
  • Won "Emerging Club of the Year" recognition
  • Established core committee structure and governance
2021

Club Foundation

  • Officially registered as APU student club in September
  • Recruited 50 passionate founding members
  • Organized first welcome social event
  • Established club vision, mission, and core values
  • Held first leadership elections
