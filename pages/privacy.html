Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Event Management Club ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or participate in our events.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

Key Points:
  • We collect only necessary information for club activities
  • Your data is never sold to third parties
  • You have control over your personal information
  • We use industry-standard security measures

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for membership or events
  • Fill out contact forms
  • Subscribe to newsletters
  • Participate in surveys or contests
  • Contact us via email or phone

This information may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Student ID number
  • University affiliation
  • Dietary preferences (for catered events)
  • Emergency contact information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of access

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Event Management: To register you for events, send reminders, and provide event-related information
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and send updates about club activities
  • Membership Management: To maintain member records and manage club operations
  • Marketing: To send newsletters and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Safety: To ensure the safety and security of events and participants
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information when you have given us explicit permission to do so.

4.2 Event Partners and Sponsors

For joint events, we may share necessary information with co-organizers or sponsors, but only with your consent.

4.3 University Administration

As a university club, we may share information with APU administration for official reporting purposes.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal processes.

4.5 Emergency Situations

We may share information if necessary to protect the safety of individuals or property.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Active Members: Data retained while membership is active
  • Inactive Members: Data retained for 2 years after last activity
  • Event Participants: Registration data retained for 1 year
  • Email Subscribers: Data retained until you unsubscribe

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (with certain exceptions)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: privacy@eventclub.edu

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with consent)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for university students aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes

Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Privacy Officer: Event Management Club
  • Email: privacy@eventclub.edu
  • General Email: info@eventclub.edu
  • Phone: +60 12-345 6789
  • Address: Event Management Club, APU Campus, Technology Park Malaysia

We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days.

13. Your Consent

By using our website and services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Thank you for trusting Event Management Club with your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with excellent service.
