Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about membership, events, and club activities

General Questions

Event Management Club is a student-run organization at APU dedicated to organizing educational, social, and professional development events. We bring together students who are interested in technology, networking, and personal growth through various workshops, competitions, and gatherings.

Any student currently enrolled at APU can join Event Management Club. We welcome students from all programs and year levels. Membership is completely free and open to everyone!

Yes! Membership is 100% free for all APU students. There are no membership fees or hidden costs. Simply register online and you're in!

Registration is easy! Visit our Register page, fill out the membership form with your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours with your membership ID and access to all club events.

Events & Activities

We organize a diverse range of events including:

  • Technical workshops (Web Development, AI, Cybersecurity, etc.)
  • Networking events with industry professionals
  • Hackathons and coding competitions
  • Social gatherings and team building activities
  • Career fairs and speaker sessions

We host 2-3 events per month on average. This includes both major events like workshops and hackathons, as well as smaller networking sessions and social gatherings. Check our Events page for the latest schedule.

Yes, while club membership gives you access to all events, you need to register for each individual event separately. This helps us plan for attendance, prepare materials, and ensure we have adequate space and resources for everyone.

No, event attendance is completely optional. While we encourage members to participate actively, there's no requirement to attend every event. Join the ones that interest you and fit your schedule!

Absolutely! We love hearing event ideas from our members. You can submit suggestions through our Contact page or speak directly with any committee member. The best ideas often come from our community!

Participation & Involvement

Yes! We always welcome volunteers to help with event planning and execution. You can join our organizing team, help with logistics, manage social media, or assist during events. It's a great way to develop leadership and organizational skills!

Committee positions are elected annually at the end of each academic year. To run for a position, you must be an active member for at least one semester and submit your candidacy during the nomination period. Elections are held democratically with all members eligible to vote.

Yes! Members receive certificates for participating in workshops, completing challenges, and contributing to club activities. These certificates can be valuable additions to your resume and portfolio.

No minimum attendance is required to maintain your membership. However, active members who regularly participate in events and contribute to the club community gain more benefits, networking opportunities, and recognition.

Technical & Logistics

Most events are held on APU campus in various lecture halls, seminar rooms, and labs. Specific locations are announced with each event registration. Occasionally, we organize off-campus events for networking and social activities.

For technical workshops, yes, bringing your own laptop is highly recommended. This allows you to follow along with hands-on exercises and keep all materials on your personal device. Event details will specify if a laptop is required.

We communicate through multiple channels:

  • Email notifications for registered members
  • Social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
  • Website event announcements
  • WhatsApp/Telegram groups for members

Follow us on social media and check your email regularly to never miss an update!

Please inform us as soon as possible if you can't attend an event you've registered for. This allows us to offer your spot to other interested students. You can notify us via email at events@apuclub.edu.my or through our contact form.
